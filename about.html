About Project Alpha

We're on a mission to make investing accessible and profitable for everyone in Bangladesh.

Who We Are

Project Alpha is a leading investment platform in Bangladesh that connects investors with profitable opportunities. Founded in 2024, we've grown to serve thousands of satisfied investors across the country.

Our platform offers a unique combination of auto-sell and self-sell options, giving our investors flexibility in how they manage their investments and maximize their returns.

We believe in transparency, security, and putting our investors first. That's why we've built a platform that's easy to use, secure, and designed to help you grow your wealth.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize investing by providing a secure, transparent, and user-friendly platform that enables everyone to build wealth through smart investments.

Our Vision

To become Bangladesh's most trusted investment platform, empowering millions of people to achieve financial freedom and security through innovative investment solutions.
